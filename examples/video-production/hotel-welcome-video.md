# Example — Boutique Hotel Welcome Video

## Input
- Client: Example Boutique Hotel
- Client type: Hotel
- Objective: Guest communication
- Audience: Arriving international guests
- Message: Welcome, explain check-in essentials, breakfast, Wi-Fi, and concierge support
- Languages: English and Dutch
- Channel: Website and WhatsApp
- Format: 16:9
- CTA: Contact the front desk or concierge

## Expected handling
The Video Production Agent must verify check-in times, breakfast hours, Wi-Fi instructions, contact details, and guest-service policies. It must provide localized scripts, pronunciation notes, visual direction, subtitles, approval gates, and guest-action KPIs.
