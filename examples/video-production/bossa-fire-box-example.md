# Reference Implementation — BOSSA Weekend Fire Box

## Client context
- Client: BOSSA Asado i Mar
- Client type: Restaurant
- Objective: Reservations and takeout orders
- Audience: Curaçao residents and weekend takeout buyers
- Language: English, with later Papiamentu, Dutch, and Spanish localization
- Channels: Instagram and WhatsApp
- Format: 9:16
- CTA: Order through WhatsApp

## Required verification
- Final box contents
- Approved price
- Ordering cutoff
- Pickup or delivery details
- Authorized product photography or B-roll

## Production rules
- Lead with fire, flavor, and weekend convenience.
- Use one clear ordering CTA.
- Do not show unapproved food items or prices.
- Record WhatsApp inquiries, orders, and attributed revenue.

## Success criteria
- Script approved in one or two review cycles
- Mobile CTA tested
- Video published with tracking ID
- Results reviewed after 24 hours and 7 days
