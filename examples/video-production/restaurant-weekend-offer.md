# Example — Restaurant Weekend Offer

## Input
- Client: Example Fire-Grill Restaurant
- Client type: Restaurant
- Objective: Reservations
- Audience: Local weekend diners
- Message: Limited weekend grill bundle
- Language: English
- Channel: Instagram and WhatsApp
- Format: 9:16
- CTA: Order on WhatsApp

## Expected handling
The Video Production Agent must request or flag:
- Verified bundle contents
- Approved price
- Ordering cutoff
- Pickup or delivery terms
- Authorized food imagery

It then returns hooks, a timed script, overlays, visual direction, subtitles, publishing copy, approval gates, and conversion KPIs.
