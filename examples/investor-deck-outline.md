# Example: Investor Deck Outline

## Input

```text
Venue: BOSSA Asado i Mar
Goal: Raise capital to open a second beachfront location
Traction: growing covers, rising average check, strong weekend reviews
Ask: amount and use of funds (expansion build-out, working capital)
```

## Expected output

- Core narrative summary (problem, concept, why now).
- Slide-by-slide outline (problem, solution, market, traction, business model, team, financials, ask).
- Draft content per slide in bullet form.
- Executive summary paragraph.
- One-pager version.
