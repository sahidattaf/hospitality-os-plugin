# Example: Customer Recovery Response

## Input

A guest leaves a review saying their main course arrived cold and service was slow during a busy Saturday at BOSSA Asado i Mar.

## Expected output

- Issue summary and severity assessment.
- Resolution offer within approval limits (e.g., complimentary dessert on next visit).
- Guest-facing apology and resolution message.
- Win-back follow-up invitation.
- Internal note with root cause (e.g., weekend kitchen staffing) and operational fix.
