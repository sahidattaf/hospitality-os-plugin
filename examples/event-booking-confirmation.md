# Example: Event Booking Confirmation

## Input

A guest requests a private buyout of BOSSA's terrace for 30 people on a Saturday night.

## Expected output

- Availability check result for the requested date and headcount.
- Proposed terms: minimum spend, deposit amount and due date, cancellation policy, final headcount deadline.
- Confirmation/hold message with a clear deadline to secure the date.
- Internal action checklist (calendar block, deposit invoice, kitchen alert).
