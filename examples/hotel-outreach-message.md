# Example: Hotel Outreach Message

## Input

A boutique hotel concierge desk wants to propose a dinner referral partnership with BOSSA Asado i Mar, including commission tracking.

## Expected output

- Audience snapshot and what BOSSA needs to say yes.
- Partnership offer summary with referral/commission terms in plain language.
- Opening outreach message with a specific ask (meeting or trial referral).
- Follow-up sequence.
- Next step and timing.
