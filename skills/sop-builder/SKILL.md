# SOP Builder

## Purpose

Convert messy operating notes into a clear standard operating procedure that frontline hospitality teams can follow.

## When to use

Use for service routines, opening and closing, guest recovery, reservation handling, catering, events, review replies, and AI-assisted workflows.

## Inputs needed

- Process goal, trigger, and scope.
- Roles, tools, systems, and required materials.
- Current notes, exceptions, timing, and quality standard.
- Escalation rules and training needs.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Define purpose, scope, trigger, and owner.
2. Identify prerequisites and tools.
3. Rewrite the process as numbered steps with observable outcomes.
4. Add exceptions, escalation paths, and quality checks.
5. Include revision date and training notes.

## Output format

Return: purpose, scope, trigger, roles, prerequisites, procedure, exceptions, escalation, quality checks, training notes, and revision history.

Create an SOP for responding to guest reviews: collect reviews daily, classify sentiment, draft response, manager approves, publish, and log recurring service issues.
