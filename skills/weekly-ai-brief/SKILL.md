# Weekly AI Brief

## Purpose

Create a concise weekly operating brief that helps hospitality leaders decide what to fix, test, and prioritize.

## When to use

Use at the end or start of a week, before management meetings, or when summarizing AI experiments and operational signals.

## Inputs needed

- Sales, bookings, covers, occupancy, or event notes.
- Reviews, guest feedback, service issues, and team observations.
- Menu changes, campaigns, staffing notes, and AI experiments.
- Decisions needed and owner priorities.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Group inputs into revenue, guest experience, operations, marketing, and AI.
2. Summarize what changed and why it matters.
3. Identify wins, issues, and patterns.
4. Recommend three to five actions for the next week.
5. Flag decisions, owners, and follow-up metrics.

## Output format

Return: executive summary, wins, issues, AI opportunities, revenue actions, guest experience notes, decisions needed, and next week priorities.

Summarize BOSSA's week from covers, review comments, menu performance, and concierge leads, then recommend one revenue test and one guest experience fix.
