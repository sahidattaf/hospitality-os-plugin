# Revenue Optimizer

## Purpose

Find practical revenue upside across pricing, capacity, packaging, channel mix, promotions, and conversion.

## When to use

Use for restaurants, boutique hotels, beach clubs, catering, tourist experiences, and events when revenue performance needs action.

## Inputs needed

- Revenue unit: covers, rooms, cabanas, events, packages, average check, or conversion.
- Demand pattern, capacity, margin, calendar, channel mix, and constraints.
- Current offers, pricing, conversion issues, and target outcome.
- Risk tolerance and test period.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Clarify the revenue lever and constraint.
2. Review demand, capacity, margin, and guest behavior.
3. Identify fast tests and structural opportunities.
4. Estimate impact qualitatively or quantitatively when data allows.
5. Define metrics, owner, and review date.

## Output format

Return: revenue diagnosis, recommended tests, pricing or package ideas, risks, metrics, owner, and next action.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

For slow midweek lunch, recommend a hotel concierge group package, pre-booked seafood platter, and cocktail attachment test rather than broad discounting.

## Test prompt

Suggest revenue tests for BOSSA to improve slow Tuesday and Wednesday lunch without discounting premium grilled items.
