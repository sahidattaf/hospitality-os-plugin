# Review Generator

## Purpose

Create warm, specific, and operationally useful responses to guest reviews and feedback.

## When to use

Use for public review replies, private guest recovery, review request campaigns, and sentiment summaries.

## Inputs needed

- Review text, rating, channel, guest name if available, and visit context.
- Brand voice, known facts, recovery policy, and escalation rules.
- Desired language and public or private response format.
- Any details that must not be mentioned.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Identify sentiment, issue type, and guest expectation.
2. Draft a response with warmth, specificity, and accountability.
3. Avoid defensiveness, invented facts, or over-promising.
4. Add service recovery action when needed.
5. Summarize internal learning and pattern to watch.

## Output format

Return: guest-facing reply, internal note, recovery action, escalation flag, and pattern to watch.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Reply to a guest who loved the grilled meats and sunset atmosphere but waited too long for dessert, thanking them and noting the team is tightening dessert pacing.

## Test prompt

Draft a public reply for a 4-star BOSSA review praising the ribeye and ocean view but saying dessert took too long.
