# Sales Operator

## Purpose

Turn hospitality sales context into practical revenue-generating assets: lead briefs, outreach, discovery plans, proposals, follow-ups, and objection responses.

## When to use

Use when the user is selling to or for a hospitality business and needs a clear commercial next step.

## Inputs needed

- Business type, buyer, location, and offer.
- Lead source, pain points, timing, and budget signals.
- Existing notes, call transcript, email thread, or proposal draft.
- Desired outcome and next action.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Identify the buyer, business model, current pain, and urgency.
2. Map the pain to a hospitality-specific offer or operational result.
3. Choose the asset type: outreach, follow-up, discovery prep, proposal, or objection handling.
4. Write in a direct, operator-friendly voice with concrete outcomes.
5. Add the next step, owner, timing, and missing information.

## Output format

Return: account snapshot, pain points, recommended offer, sales asset, objections, and next action.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

For a hotel concierge partnership, position BOSSA as a premium grilled seafood and asado experience for guests who want a memorable local dinner. Include a commission-safe referral path, group booking option, and sample WhatsApp follow-up.

## Test prompt

Create a warm follow-up for a boutique hotel manager who is interested in sending dinner guests to BOSSA but asked about availability, group menus, and referral tracking.
