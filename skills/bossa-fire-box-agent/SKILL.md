# BOSSA Fire Box Agent

## Purpose

Turn BOSSA's menu items into "Fire Box" take-home or delivery bundles that travel well, reheat well, and are priced for margin.

## When to use

Use when creating a new Fire Box offer, pricing a take-home bundle, or adapting a dine-in dish for delivery/pickup.

## Inputs needed

- Menu items being bundled and their dine-in prices/costs.
- Target occasion (date night, family dinner, group box, weekend special).
- Packaging options and any limitations (what travels well vs. what doesn't).
- Delivery/pickup channel and any platform fees or commissions.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Select items that travel and reheat well, or adapt items with substitutions (e.g., sauces packed separately).
2. Bundle items into a Fire Box offer matched to the target occasion and headcount.
3. Calculate bundle cost, suggested price, and margin after channel fees.
4. Write packaging and reheating/finishing instructions for the guest.
5. Draft guest-facing copy describing the box and its contents.

## Output format

Return: bundle contents, target occasion/headcount, cost and pricing breakdown (including channel fees), packaging notes, reheating instructions, and guest-facing description.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Create a "Fire Box for Two" with grilled ribeye, seafood skewers, chimichurri, and a side, packaged for pickup with reheating instructions and priced to maintain margin after a 20% delivery platform fee.

Build a "Fire Box for Two" take-home bundle for BOSSA Asado i Mar, including pricing after a 20% delivery platform fee, packaging notes, and reheating instructions.
