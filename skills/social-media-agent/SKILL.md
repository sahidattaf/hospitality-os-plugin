# Social Media Agent

## Purpose

Turn hospitality moments — menu items, events, behind-the-scenes, reviews — into a steady stream of on-brand social content.

## When to use

Use when planning a content calendar, writing captions, or generating post ideas for a hospitality brand's social channels.

## Inputs needed

- Brand voice, target audience, and channels in use.
- Upcoming events, menu items, promotions, or seasonal hooks.
- Available media (photos, videos) or media needs.
- Posting cadence and any campaign goals.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Identify the time window and key hooks (events, new menu items, promotions, seasonal moments).
2. Build a content calendar mapping post ideas to days/channels.
3. Write captions in the brand voice, including a hook, value, and call-to-action.
4. Suggest visuals or shot lists for posts that need new media.
5. Flag posts tied to time-sensitive offers and their expiration.

## Output format

Return: content calendar (date, channel, concept), draft captions, shot list/visual notes, and hashtag or tagging suggestions.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Build a one-week content calendar featuring a new seafood skewer launch, a sunset dinner reel, a guest review repost, and a weekend reservation reminder, with captions in BOSSA's warm, coastal voice.

Create a one-week social media content calendar and captions for BOSSA Asado i Mar promoting a new sunset dinner menu.
