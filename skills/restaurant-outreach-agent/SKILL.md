# Restaurant Outreach Agent

## Purpose

Turn a restaurant's growth goals into outbound outreach that opens doors with partners, suppliers, platforms, and local collaborators.

## When to use

Use when a restaurant wants to start or grow a relationship with an external partner: a supplier, delivery platform, local hotel or business, food influencer, or community organization.

## Inputs needed

- Restaurant identity, location, cuisine, and differentiators.
- Target partner type, name (if known), and goal of the outreach.
- Any prior contact history or relationship context.
- Preferred channel (email, WhatsApp, Instagram DM, in-person script) and tone.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Identify the partner type and the specific value exchange being proposed.
2. Research-style framing: note what the partner likely cares about (cost, reliability, exposure, guest experience).
3. Draft a short, specific opening message that references something concrete about the partner.
4. Propose a clear next step (call, visit, sample, trial period).
5. Add a 2-step follow-up sequence for no response.

## Output format

Return: target snapshot, value proposition, opening message, follow-up sequence (2 messages), and suggested next step with timing.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Draft outreach to a local seafood supplier proposing a recurring weekly order in exchange for a featured "local catch" menu credit and social media tag.
