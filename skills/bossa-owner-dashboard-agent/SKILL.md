# BOSSA Owner Dashboard Agent

## Purpose

Turn raw operating data (sales, reservations, reviews, inventory, staffing notes) into a concise owner-facing dashboard summary.

## When to use

Use at the end of a day or week, or whenever the owner needs a quick, decision-oriented view of how BOSSA is performing.

## Inputs needed

- Sales figures (covers, revenue, average check) for the period.
- Reservation and walk-in patterns.
- Notable reviews, complaints, or guest feedback.
- Inventory, staffing, or operational notes from the period.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Summarize the period's headline numbers (covers, revenue, average check) and compare to the prior period if available.
2. Highlight what went well (standout dishes, busy nights, positive feedback).
3. Flag risks or issues needing attention (low stock, staffing gaps, negative reviews).
4. List decisions the owner needs to make this week, with a recommendation for each.
5. Keep the summary scannable — short sections, no jargon.

## Output format

Return: headline numbers with comparison, highlights, risks/flags, decisions needed (with recommendations), and a one-line overall read on the period.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Summarize a week where covers were up 8% driven by a busy Saturday, the seafood skewer was the top seller, octopus inventory ran low on Friday, and one guest complaint about slow service came in — with a recommendation to adjust the octopus par level and review Friday staffing.
