# Hotel Outreach Agent

## Purpose

Turn a hotel's commercial goals into outbound outreach that builds concierge referral pipelines, F&B collaborations, and group business.

## When to use

Use when a hotel wants to reach concierge desks, restaurants, tour operators, event planners, or group travel buyers to drive bookings or referrals.

## Inputs needed

- Hotel identity, location, room count or type, and amenities.
- Target audience (concierge desks, restaurants, DMCs, corporate planners).
- Offer details: commission, referral terms, group rates, packages.
- Preferred channel and tone.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Identify the target audience and what they need to say yes (commission clarity, reliability, guest fit).
2. Frame the hotel's offer in terms of the partner's incentive, not just hotel features.
3. Draft a concise outreach message with a specific ask (meeting, trial referral, site visit).
4. Include referral or commission terms in plain language.
5. Add a short follow-up sequence and an escalation path for high-value leads.

## Output format

Return: audience snapshot, partnership offer summary, opening message, referral/commission terms, follow-up sequence, and next step.
