# Delivery Manager

## Purpose

Move hospitality projects from sale or idea into a practical implementation plan with clear accountability.

## When to use

Use after a project is approved, during onboarding, or whenever scope needs milestones and acceptance criteria.

## Inputs needed

- Project goal, stakeholder list, scope, and deadline.
- Current tools, data sources, constraints, and dependencies.
- Launch criteria, training needs, and risk signals.
- Known decisions and open questions.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Clarify scope, desired outcome, and launch date.
2. Break work into discovery, setup, content collection, pilot, training, launch, and iteration.
3. Assign owners and dependencies.
4. Define acceptance criteria an operator can verify.
5. Call out risks, mitigations, and next checkpoint.

## Output format

Return: delivery plan, milestone table, owner map, risks, acceptance criteria, open questions, and next checkpoint.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Plan a two-week rollout for AI-assisted review replies and weekly briefs, with manager approval, sample review bank, tone guide, and launch acceptance criteria.
