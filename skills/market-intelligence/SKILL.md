# Market Intelligence

## Purpose

Turn market, competitor, guest, and tourism signals into actions a hospitality operator can use.

## When to use

Use for competitive scans, positioning, local demand checks, event-driven planning, pricing context, or trend briefs.

## Inputs needed

- Market, geography, segment, and decision horizon.
- Competitors, channels, guest segments, and known assumptions.
- Internal performance data or questions to answer.
- Whether latest external research is required.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Confirm geography, segment, and time horizon.
2. Gather current sources when facts may have changed.
3. Separate facts, assumptions, and interpretation.
4. Identify opportunities, risks, and competitor gaps.
5. Recommend operator actions with confidence level.

## Output format

Return: market snapshot, signals, competitor notes, guest demand, opportunities, risks, recommended actions, and sources when used.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Scan local dining and tourism signals to decide whether BOSSA should promote a sunset group grill package, midweek lunch offer, or concierge partnership.
