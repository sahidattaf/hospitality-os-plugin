# Decision Log

## Purpose

Record important operating decisions so future teammates understand what was decided, why, and when to revisit it.

## When to use

Use after pricing, menu, staffing, vendor, campaign, policy, or AI workflow decisions.

## Inputs needed

- Decision statement and date.
- Context, constraints, options considered, and rationale.
- Owner, stakeholders, expected impact, and review date.
- Conditions that would change the decision.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. State the decision in one plain sentence.
2. Capture context and constraints.
3. List options considered and why they were accepted or rejected.
4. Record owner, follow-up date, and expected impact.
5. Add reversal triggers and related links or notes.

## Output format

Return: decision, date, owner, context, options considered, rationale, expected impact, reversal triggers, follow-up date, and related notes.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Log the decision to test a fixed-price group lunch menu for hotel concierge referrals for four weeks before adding it permanently.
