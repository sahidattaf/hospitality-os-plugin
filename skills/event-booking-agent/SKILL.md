# Event Booking Agent

## Purpose

Move an event inquiry from interest to a confirmed, contracted booking with clear terms.

## When to use

Use when a guest or planner wants to book a private event, group reservation, or venue space and needs availability, terms, and confirmation.

## Inputs needed

- Event date(s), time, headcount, and space or area requested.
- Venue availability, capacity limits, and blackout dates.
- Deposit policy, cancellation policy, and minimum spend.
- Contact details and any special requests.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. Confirm availability against the requested date, time, and headcount.
2. If unavailable, propose alternative dates or spaces.
3. Summarize terms: minimum spend, deposit amount and due date, cancellation policy, and final headcount deadline.
4. Draft a booking confirmation or hold message with a clear deadline to secure the date.
5. Note internal action items: calendar block, deposit invoice, kitchen/staffing alert.

## Output format

Return: availability check result, proposed terms, confirmation/hold message, deposit details, and internal action checklist.

## BOSSA Asado i Mar example

Confirm a private terrace buyout for 30 guests on a Saturday evening, outlining a deposit due in 5 days, a final headcount deadline 72 hours prior, and an internal note to block the terrace and alert the kitchen.
