# Hospitality OS Video Approval Policy

## Mandatory approval gates

### 1. Commercial gate
Confirm the offer, price, date, availability, capacity, location, opening hours, ingredients, and service terms are accurate or omitted.

### 2. Brand gate
Confirm tone, logo, imagery, visual identity, guest promise, and prohibited claims match the client brand.

### 3. Language gate
Confirm localization, spelling, pronunciation, subtitles, and cultural fit with an approved reviewer.

### 4. Identity gate
Confirm explicit authorization for every real-person avatar, digital twin, and cloned voice used in production.

### 5. Production gate
Confirm lip sync, audio, pacing, subtitles, safe margins, B-roll, music rights, and CTA visibility.

### 6. Release gate
Confirm the destination URL works on mobile, tracking exists, final approval is recorded, and the approved channel and date are correct.

## Approval roles
- Internal reviewer: Hospitality OS operator or delegated producer
- Client reviewer: client owner or authorized marketing contact
- Language reviewer: approved speaker or translator
- Final release owner: named person accountable for publication

## No-release conditions
Do not publish when:
- Facts remain unverified
- Avatar or voice consent is unclear
- Client approval is missing
- Language review is incomplete
- CTA destination is broken
- Asset licensing is uncertain
- The video contains deceptive testimonials, unsupported guarantees, or fabricated claims

## Approval record
Store:
- Script version
- Language version
- Draft asset URL
- Reviewer names
- Approval dates
- Revision notes
- Final approved file or URL
