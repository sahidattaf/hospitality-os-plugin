# Hospitality OS Video KPI Framework

## Objective-to-KPI mapping

| Objective | Primary KPI | Supporting KPIs |
|---|---|---|
| Reservations | Confirmed bookings | Clicks, inquiries, booking conversion |
| Catering | Qualified catering leads | Proposal requests, booked value, revenue |
| Private events | Event inquiries | Discovery calls, proposals, conversion |
| Tourist awareness | Visits or reservations | Reach, completion rate, profile actions |
| Hotel guest communication | Guest action completion | Link clicks, front-desk contacts, satisfaction |
| Staff training | Training completion | Quiz score, manager sign-off, incident reduction |
| Customer recovery | Recovered guest | Response rate, return visit, review update |
| Demo and sales | Qualified opportunity | Demo requests, proposals, closed revenue |

## Measurement windows
- 24 hours: delivery and early creative signal
- 7 days: conversion signal
- 30 days: revenue and reusable-learning signal

## Core formulas

```text
Hook Rate = 3-second viewers / video starts
Completion Rate = completed views / video starts
Click-through Rate = destination clicks / impressions
Lead Conversion = qualified leads / destination clicks
Booking Conversion = confirmed bookings / qualified leads
Revenue per Video = attributed revenue / published videos
```

## Decision rules
- High reach and low completion: replace the opening or shorten the video.
- High completion and low clicks: strengthen the CTA or offer.
- High clicks and low leads: fix the landing page, WhatsApp flow, or message clarity.
- High leads and low bookings: improve follow-up and sales qualification.
- Strong conversion: reuse the format and localize into approved languages.

## Required learning note
Every measured campaign must record:
- Winning hook
- Best audience
- Best language
- Best channel
- CTA result
- Commercial outcome
- Next experiment
