# Hospitality Video Approval Checklist

## Commercial accuracy
- Offer matches the approved brief
- Price, dates, availability, capacity, location, and service terms are verified or omitted
- No unsupported awards, reviews, guarantees, health claims, or performance claims

## Language and delivery
- Language matches the target audience
- Pronunciation is approved
- Spoken delivery sounds natural
- Subtitle spelling, timing, and safe margins are correct

## Brand and identity
- Correct logo, visual identity, and brand tone
- Food, rooms, venues, and services shown accurately
- Avatar and voice use are authorized
- Music, images, and footage are licensed or approved

## Conversion
- One primary CTA
- CTA matches the objective
- Destination tested on mobile
- Tracking identifier included
